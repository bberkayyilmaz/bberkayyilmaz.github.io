About Me

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology. I joined Aether Argus Inc. in 2020 as a Lead Signal Processing Engineer and Researcher. I currently serve as an Assistant Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Wayne State University. My research interests include electromagnetic side-channel analysis, signal processing, hardware security, communications, and machine learning.

Currently, our team is dedicated to enhancing supply chain security by developing non-intrusive, non-destructive methodologies that leverage EM side-channels. Although we lack control over the power of the emitted signals, we work to maximize the reliability and accuracy of our approach.
